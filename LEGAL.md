# Legal Policy

ARC-SeedCatalog is intended for lawful catalog proof workflows.

## Allowed

- Public-domain catalogs
- Licensed APIs
- Owned/internal inventories
- Homebrew/demo catalogs
- Synthetic test feeds
- Authorized dataset indexes

## Disallowed

- Piracy-site mirroring
- Unauthorized streaming route indexing
- Hidden host/server preservation
- Raw stream URL export
- Access-control bypass
- User tracking without consent
- Exporting raw titles, URLs, paths, hostnames, tokens, cookies, or user identifiers

The system is designed so raw fields may be present in input, but exported proof bundles do not preserve them.
